National Hockey League

Introduction

The National Hockey League, or NHL, currently consists of 31 teams. The 32nd team will be based in Seattle, WA and will join the league in the 2021-2022 season.


Interesting Fact

The original six hockey teams still exist today, they are the Detroit Red Wings, Chicago Blackhawks, Boston Bruins, Toronto Maple Leafs, Montreal Canadiens, and the New York Rangers

Divisions

The NHL is divided into 4 divisions:

  • Atlantic
  • Central
  • Metropolitan
  • Pacific

Due to COVID-19, the NHL consisted of the North, West, Central, and East divisions.
